Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Most people infected with the virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment.

protect now

take steps to protect yourself

Wear A Face Mask

Masks are made to contain droplets and particles that you breathe, cough, or sneeze out. A variety of masks are available. Some masks provide a higher level of protection than others.

learn more

Wash Your Hands

Keeping hands clean helps prevent the spread of germs,like COVID-19.Regular handwashing is one of the best ways to remove germs, avoid getting sick, and prevent the spread of germs to others.

learn more

Avoid Close Contact

Avoiding contact with people who have COVID-19, whether or not they feel sick, can reduce your risk of catching the virus from them. If possible, avoid being around a person who has COVID-19 until they can safely end home isolation.
